revzio homepage product demo — replace these files
==================================================

CURRENT PLACEHOLDER (from maxima.ai — swap before launch):
  Video:  media/hero-demo.mp4
  Poster: media/hero-demo-poster.png

TO USE YOUR OWN DEMO:
  1. Export your screen recording as MP4 (recommended: 1920×1200 or 16:10, under 15 MB).
  2. Save it as:  media/hero-demo.mp4  (overwrite the placeholder)
  3. Export a still frame as PNG:  media/hero-demo-poster.png  (shown before video loads)
  4. Refresh http://localhost:8000/

OPTIONAL — GIF instead of video:
  In index.html, replace the <video> block with:
    <img src="media/hero-demo.gif" alt="revzio product demo" class="product-showcase__media">

OPTIONAL — host on CDN instead of local file:
  Change the <source> src in index.html to your URL, e.g.:
    <source src="https://your-cdn.com/revzio-demo.mp4" type="video/mp4">
