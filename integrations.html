INTEGRATION HIGHLIGHTS

Featured integrations.

Where Orion goes deepest: ERP posting, real-time billing sync, and accounting system workflows.

◎

NetSuite

Orion connects directly to NetSuite via SuiteQL and REST APIs. Journal entries post automatically, reconciliation data syncs in real time, and the close checklist pulls entity data without manual exports.

◎

Stripe

The Orion Reconciliation and Revenue Validation agents pull Stripe billing events, charges, refunds, and disputes in real time — matching against your ERP without any CSV exports.

◎

QuickBooks

Orion supports QuickBooks Online and Desktop with native sync for journal entries, reconciliation, and close management — ideal for growing teams before a NetSuite migration.
