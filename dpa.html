Scope

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of your agreement with revzio when you require GDPR or similar data protection terms for personal data processed through the service.

Roles

You act as the data controller for customer data you upload. revzio acts as data processor, processing personal data only on your documented instructions.

Subprocessors

We use vetted subprocessors for hosting and operations. A current list is available on request at trust@revzio.com. We notify customers of material changes.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures as described in our Security page and SOC 2 report.

Data subject rights

We assist you in responding to data subject requests where personal data is processed on your behalf.

Contact

For a signed DPA or subprocessors list: trust@revzio.com.
