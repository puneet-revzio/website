Ingest, extract, match, route — with exceptions surfaced instantly.

1. Ingest from anywhere

Orion fetches invoices from email, vendor portals, ERP submissions, and web forms automatically. No manual collection.

2. Extract and standardize

AI vision models extract line-item data, standardize formats, and map each line to the correct GL account — regardless of invoice layout.

3. Run 3-way match

Orion matches PO, GRN, and invoice automatically. Discrepancies and duplicates are flagged instantly with clear explanations.

4. Route and pay

Valid invoices route to the right approver based on amount and category. Approved invoices sync to your ERP for on-time payment.
