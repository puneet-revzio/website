Define revenue integrity

Revenue integrity means your reported numbers tie to source systems, policies, and approvals — with evidence you can produce on demand. Controllers often own the outcome but inherit fragmented tooling.

Metrics that actually predict pain

Track exception rate by stream, mean time to resolve, late adjustments after soft close, and manual journal volume. Trend them monthly; spikes precede audit findings.

Ownership map

Every reconciliation stream needs a preparer, reviewer, and escalation path. Ambiguity is where month-end stalls. Document it once, enforce it in workflow software.
