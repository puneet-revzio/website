AI in accounting: agency over automation

The best finance AI does not replace judgment. It gives accountants agency — faster evidence, clearer options, defensible outcomes.

Read article

April 28, 2026 · 6 min read

Automation vs agency

Automation executes rules. Agency means the accountant chooses among options with full context — standards cited, sources linked, overrides logged. Finance regulators and auditors care about the latter.

What to demand from vendors

Ask for traceability to source documents, role-based approvals, and clear boundaries on what the model inferred vs what a human confirmed. Black-box matching fails scrutiny.
