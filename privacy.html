Overview

revzio, Inc. ("revzio," "we," "us") provides reconciliation and close workflow software for finance teams. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle personal data when you use our website, platform, and related services.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide (name, work email, company, phone), usage data from the platform, and technical data such as IP address and device information. We do not sell personal information.

How we use information

We use data to provide and improve the service, communicate with you, ensure security, and comply with legal obligations. Marketing communications require your consent where required by law.

Sharing

We share data with subprocessors who help us operate the service (hosting, analytics, support), under contractual safeguards. We may disclose information if required by law.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data. Contact contact@revzio.ai to exercise these rights.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact@revzio.ai.
