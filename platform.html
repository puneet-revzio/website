ORION RECONCILIATION ENGINE

Matched at the line-item level. Explained in plain English.

The Orion Reconciliation Agent doesn't just match invoice totals — it matches every line, every tax breakdown, every promo credit, across bank, GL, and subledger simultaneously. Breaks surface with a plain-English explanation and suggested resolution — not a raw variance number.

◆

Pattern-matched

Orion learns your business logic and generates matching rules automatically. No re-explaining the same patterns every cycle.

○

Variance explained

Every exception arrives with a summary: what broke, why, the likely cause, and what your team should check first.

△

Continuously running

Reconciliation runs in real time — not just at month-end. Issues surface the day they happen, not week three of close.

ORION CLOSE MANAGEMENT

From pre-close checklist to controller sign-off — orchestrated by Orion.

The Orion Close Management Agent replaces email chains, status spreadsheets, and "is this done?" Slack threads with a structured, automated close workflow. Every task is owned, tracked, and connected to the evidence it needs.

◐

Standardized playbooks

Templates per entity and close type. The same close, the same way, every month — with Orion tracking every step.

◇

Blockers surfaced

Know which task is stuck, who owns it, and how pace compares to last cycle — before it becomes a crisis.

✦

Sign-off in product

Reviewers approve where the work lives. The audit trail writes itself. No PDFs, no email threads.

AUDITABLE AI

Designed for auditors from day one.

Every Orion agent action — every match, every journal entry, every exception resolution — is captured with full context: source data, agent logic, reviewer identity, timestamp, and outcome. When external audit arrives, you hand them a report, not a reconstruction project.

★

SOC 2 Type II

Independently audited controls. Full security report available on day one of your evaluation.

⬢

Role-based access

Granular permissions for preparers, reviewers, and approvers. Mirrors your existing control matrix with zero configuration.

⬡

Versioned trail

Every agent action is immutable and versioned. Nothing is truly deleted. Everything is explainable to any auditor.
