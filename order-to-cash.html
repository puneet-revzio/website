HOW ORION HANDLES O2C

Agent-prepared AR operations in four steps.

Orion automates the prep work end-to-end — matching, prioritizing, routing, and documenting — so your team focuses on exceptions.

STEP 01

Invoice to payment matching

Orion AR auto-matches incoming payments to open invoices using remittance data, bank references, and pattern matching. 90%+ accuracy from day one.

STEP 02

Collections prioritization

Open AR ranked by ageing, invoice value, and customer payment history. Collectors always work the highest-impact accounts first.

STEP 03

Automated follow-up sequences

Payment reminders, escalations, and promise-to-pay tracking run on schedule. No manual initiation needed.

STEP 04

Dispute resolution routing

Disputed invoices and deductions routed with full context — amount, aging, prior communications — for faster resolution.
