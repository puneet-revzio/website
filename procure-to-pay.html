HOW ORION P2P WORKS

Four steps from inbox to ERP.

Orion prepares the work continuously — extraction, matching, routing — and your team approves exceptions.

STEP 01

Fetch from anywhere

Orion AP collects invoices from email, ERP submissions, vendor portals, and web forms automatically on a continuous schedule.

STEP 02

Extract and standardize

AI vision models extract line-item data from any invoice format — PDFs, scans, structured files — and map each line to the correct GL account.

STEP 03

Run 3-way match

PO, GRN, and invoice automatically matched. Discrepancies, duplicates, and outliers flagged instantly with clear explanations.

STEP 04

Route and close

Valid invoices route to the right approver by amount, vendor, or category. Approved invoices post directly to ERP. Payment scheduled on time.
