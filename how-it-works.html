THE ORION WORKFLOW

From raw data to audit-ready output in four steps.

Orion connects your stack, builds a unified data layer, runs agents continuously, and routes decisions to the right reviewers with evidence attached.

Step 01

Connect your finance stack

Orion connects to your ERP, billing system, bank feeds, payroll, and CRM through 40+ native integrations. Data flows in automatically on a continuous schedule — no CSV exports, no manual uploads, no IT project required.

Typical connection time: under 3 weeks
Step 02

Build your unified data layer

All source data is normalized, enriched, and unified into a single finance data layer. Orion resolves entity names, account mappings, and currency conversions automatically — so every agent operates from the same clean, consistent foundation.

One source of truth across all systems
Step 03

Agents run continuously

Each Orion agent operates autonomously within its domain — matching transactions, generating journal entries, processing invoices, validating revenue — around the clock. Agents flag exceptions, draft explanations, and route work to the right human reviewer.

Human-in-the-loop at every critical decision point
Step 04

Your team reviews and approves

Everything Orion prepares is surfaced for review with full context: source data, agent logic, and a clear recommendation. Reviewers approve, adjust, or escalate. Every decision is logged with evidence — automatically captured for audit.

Sign-off in product. Audit trail writes itself.
WHAT POWERS ORION

Three layers. Built for finance-grade precision.

Orion is engineered for explainability, controls, and continuous outputs across real finance workflows.

⛁

Finance Data Layer

Orion's data layer unifies every source into a single, continuously-updated model of your financial activity. Entity resolution, account mapping, FX conversion, and data quality checks run automatically — so agents always operate on clean data.

  • Native integrations
  • Real-time sync
  • Data lineage
  • Master data management
⟡

Specialized Agent Engine

Each Orion agent is purpose-built for its finance workflow — not a general-purpose AI model adapted for finance. Reconciliation logic, journal entry generation, AP matching, and revenue validation each require fundamentally different approaches.

  • 7 specialized agents
  • Deterministic logic
  • Continuous validation
  • Exception routing

Governed Workflow Layer

Every agent operates within a governed workflow layer — policy thresholds, approval routing, role-based access, and immutable audit logging. Orion prepares; your team approves. Nothing posts to your ERP without human sign-off.

  • Human-in-the-loop
  • Policy-gated
  • SOX-ready
  • Full audit trail
